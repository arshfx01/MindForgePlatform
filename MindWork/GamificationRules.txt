PROGRESSION SYSTEM

1. Experience Points (XP)

Base Reward: Score x 2.

Streak Bonus: +10% XP for every day in a streak (caps at +50%).

First-Time Clear: +100 XP bonus.

2. Levels & Titles

Level 1-5: "The Apprentice" (Unlocks basic logic puzzles).

Level 6-15: "The Analyst" (Unlocks business & economic crises).

Level 16-30: "The Strategist" (Unlocks global geopolitical dilemmas).

Level 31+: "The Sage" (User can now "Peer Review" others).

3. Badges (The "Dopamine Hits")

"Fallacy Hunter": Find 5 different fallacies in scenarios.

"Devil's Advocate": Submit a high-scoring argument for a position you personally disagree with.

"Iron Logic": Score 95+ on three consecutive scenarios.

4. The Daily Dilemma

One scenario per day that offers 2x XP.

Global leaderboard resets weekly to keep competition fresh for new users.
